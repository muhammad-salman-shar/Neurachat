# **App Name**: NeuraSaMu: Your AI Companion

## Core Features:

- Multi-Agent Interaction: Multi-Agent System: Interact with different AI agents (Friend, Teacher, Boss, etc.), each with unique personalities and behaviors. Grok, Gemini, and GPT-4o are used as tools in order to respond accurately, and without hallucination.
- Group Interaction: Group Chat: WhatsApp-style group chats where users can interact with AI agents and real friends simultaneously.
- Emotional Intelligence: Emotion Detection: Analyze user chat tone and images to detect emotions and trigger appropriate responses from AI agents. The LLM acts as a tool and reasons whether or not to act on its emotion detections.
- Multimedia & Utility Features: Multimedia Sharing: Share photos and other media. Review photo content to add honest, fun comments.
- Personality Memory: Agent Memory: Each AI agent maintains a separate memory log to personalize interactions and recall past conversations.
- Smart Notifications: Smart Notifications: Receive personalized alerts, task reminders, and auto-calls from AI agents. Users can customize notification preferences.
- User preferences: Customizable Interface: Toggle on/off the smart notifications. Option for users to choose different cloud backends to suit their security/privacy needs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: HSL 210, 65%, 50% (approximates to RGB hex #3FA7FF). A bright and friendly blue to convey trustworthiness and innovation.
- Background color: HSL 210, 20%, 95% (approximates to RGB hex #F0F8FF). A light, desaturated blue that provides a clean backdrop, ensuring readability and focus on content.
- Accent color: HSL 180, 55%, 55% (approximates to RGB hex #4DD0E1). A contrasting cyan to highlight interactive elements and important information, drawing the user's eye to key features.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif font known for its modern look and readability, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use a consistent set of modern and friendly icons for navigation and actions, with a slight emphasis on rounded shapes and a touch of detail for clarity.
- Employ a clean, card-based layout with intuitive navigation, mimicking familiar chat apps while integrating AI agent interactions smoothly.
- Incorporate subtle animations for agent responses and notification alerts, enhancing the sense of interactivity and making the experience more engaging.
